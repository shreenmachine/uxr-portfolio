About Me

A user’s journey to your website can begin many ways, whether it be through a search engine, an advertisement, email subscription, or even a recommendation from a friend. That journey doesn’t end once they reach your site, though. That’s where my team comes in.

Any journey on your site can be helped or hindered along the way. I use quantitative data analysis to identify ways to optimize and streamline a user’s journey as they interact with the site’s search bar, navigation structure, product refinements, or marketing content. In situations where there is no discernable data to assess why a user may be behaving a certain way, I look to solicit real-time feedback. Methods like moderated or moderated usability testing, A/B testing, tree-testing, card-sorting, and survey analysis help identify the reasoning behind behaviour, which in turn helps me make well-informed decisions.

After signing off with key stakeholders on a strategy change, I work cross-functionally to implement best-in-class, future-oriented strategy. I take a multi-faceted approach to this challenge, be it working with merchants and category managers on competitive analyses of similar products and businesses, working with the SEO team to optimize folder hierarchy and naming conventions, or collaborating with front-end web development to improve UX/UI design. If you’re interested in learning more about what I’ve done, check out my portfolio or contact me— I’d love to hear from you!
